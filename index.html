About Me

I am a full stack developer with experience designing and deploying scalable web applications using the MERN stack, Django, and cloud platforms. I enjoy building secure APIs, dynamic interfaces, and information systems that solve real problems for users.

I am completing my Bachelor of Science in Information Science at the University of South Florida, with coursework that connects web development, information policy, security, and user experience.

Enhanced Biography and Career Goals

My journey into information science has been shaped by both school and work, and the two have blended together in ways that define how I approach problems today. I am finishing my BS in Information Science at USF with a focus on web design technologies, while working as a full stack software engineer. Having that mix of academic learning and professional experience has helped me grow as a developer and as a problem solver.

Early courses such as Introduction to Information Science and IT Concepts for Information Professionals gave me a foundation for understanding how information moves through organizations and systems, including ethics and accessibility. Later classes in interaction design, information architecture, and information policy pushed me to think about usability, structure, and the social impact of technology, not just the code behind it.

Professionally, I have applied these ideas by building features for a project management platform at ActionAtlas using React, Node.js, and MongoDB, and by working on custom applications as a self-employed engineer. Looking ahead, I hope to continue in roles that blend development with leadership, such as full stack engineer, solutions architect, or project lead, while staying grounded in ethical and human-centered design.
